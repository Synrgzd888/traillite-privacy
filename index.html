TrailLite Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026

TrailLite ("we", "us", the "app") is designed to be private by default. This policy explains what information the app handles and how. TrailLite has no user accounts, no login, and no server or cloud database of its own.

Information stored on your device

Your workouts, recorded routes, activity history, and app settings are stored only in local storage on your device. This information is not transmitted to us, is not synced to any cloud service, and never leaves your device unless you choose to share it yourself. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

Location

TrailLite uses your device's location only while you are actively recording a workout (walk, run, or ride), so it can draw your route and show your position on the map. Location recording continues while the screen is locked so your route keeps recording in your pocket, and stops the moment you end the workout. TrailLite does not access your location when you are not tracking.

Your precise route data stays on your device. When you use the Discover feature to search for destinations or explore nearby trails, the app sends your typed search text and an approximate (coarse) location — together with the map area you are viewing — to the OpenStreetMap Foundation's services (Nominatim and Overpass) in order to return relevant places and trails. These are standard web requests that contain no account information and no advertising identifiers. As with any internet request, your device's IP address is visible to the server handling the request. TrailLite does not store these searches.

Purchases

TrailLite offers an optional subscription, TrailLite Plus. Payments are processed by Apple through the App Store — TrailLite never sees or stores your payment card details. To manage subscription entitlements (i.e. to know whether TrailLite Plus is active), the app uses RevenueCat, a subscription management service. RevenueCat receives your purchase history and an anonymous, randomly generated identifier associated with your purchases. It does not receive your name, email address, or payment details, and this information is not used for advertising or tracking.

What we do not do

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or contacts.
  • We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not collect advertising identifiers.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with data brokers.

Third-party services

TrailLite relies on the following third parties for the functions described above:

  • Apple App Store — payment processing and subscription management.
  • RevenueCat — subscription entitlement management (purchase history, anonymous identifier).
  • OpenStreetMap Foundation (Nominatim / Overpass) — destination search and trail data for the Discover feature.
  • Apple Maps (MapKit) — map display on iOS, provided by the operating system.

Each third party handles data in accordance with its own privacy policy.

Children

TrailLite is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

You can delete individual workouts within the app at any time, and uninstalling the app erases all locally stored data. You can manage or cancel your TrailLite Plus subscription through your Apple ID settings.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent version.
